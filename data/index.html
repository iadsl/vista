Data Resources

A collection of crime data sources for spatiotemporal analytics and research

  • Chicago Crime Data The Chicago Police Department provides detailed and extensive crime data, including information on reported crimes dating back to 2001. This dataset is ideal for analyzing urban crime trends and patterns.
    • Website: Chicago Data Portal
    • Direct Link to Crime Data:
      • Crimes - 2001 to Present

  • New York City Crime Data The NYC Open Data portal, maintained by the NYPD, offers detailed crime data. These datasets provide historical as well as current year-to-date records of reported incidents across New York City.

    • Website: NYC Open Data
    • Direct Links to NYPD Crime Data:
      • NYPD Complaint Data Historic (2006–2019)
      • NYPD Complaint Data Current (2020–Present)

  • Los Angeles Crime Data The City of Los Angeles Open Data Portal, maintained by the LAPD, provides detailed crime data. This dataset includes records of crimes reported since 2010 and covers a variety of crime types.

    • Website: Los Angeles Open Data Portal
    • Direct Link to LAPD Crime Data:
      • Crime Data from 2010 to Present
  • Portland Crime Data
    The Portland Police Bureau provides detailed and extensive crime data, including information on reported crimes and trends. This dataset is valuable for analyzing crime patterns across Portland neighborhoods.
    • Website: Portland Police Bureau Open Data
    • Direct Links to Crime Data:
      • Calls for Service
      • Geospatial Open Data Portal
  • Grand Rapids Crime Data
    The Grand Rapids Police Department shares crime data through its transparency dashboard. This data includes reports on crime trends, calls for service, and other safety-related information for the city.
    • Website: Grand Rapids Police Department
    • Direct Links to Crime Data:
      • GRPD Data
  • Detroit Crime Data
    The Detroit Police Department offers access to crime data via its open data portal, including detailed crime reports and public safety metrics. This dataset is ideal for understanding crime trends in Detroit.
    • Website: Detroit Open Data Portal
    • Direct Links to Crime Data:
      • Reported Crimes in Detroit
      • Crime Viewer

  • UK Crime Data The UK Police Data portal aggregates crime data for England, Wales, and Northern Ireland. This resource includes data categorized by police forces and provides insights into crime outcomes and trends.

    • Website: UK Police Data Portal
    • Direct Links:
      • Explore and Download Crime Data
